Intro

This section introduces a variety of research and application areas that intersect with Human-Computer Interaction (HCI). Topics covered reflect personal and academic interests that include accessibility, emerging technologies, ethics in design, and user experience strategies. These themes represent how HCI adapts and responds to current social and technological changes.

Explore the dropdown menu to learn more about specialized subtopics through curated resources, short videos, and recorded screencasts. Each area highlights a key dimension of HCI that can shape user research and practical application across sectors including education, healthcare, public service, and emerging media.
